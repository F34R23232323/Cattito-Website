Achievement System

Cattito's achievement system tracks your progress, from catching cats to completing storylines and performing various commands or actions. Use /achievements to view your achievements and categories. Hidden achievements only appear once unlocked, e.g., "69/90 + 5".

Categories (95 achievements)

Cat Hunt - 17 achievements: story and cat-catching related
Commands - 12 achievements: run commands to earn achievements
Random - 17 achievements: fun/joke achievements
Silly - 24 achievements: funny achievements, sometimes more difficult
Hard - 20 achievements: challenging, requiring effort, time, luck
Hidden - 5 achievements: extremely difficult/unexpected actions
