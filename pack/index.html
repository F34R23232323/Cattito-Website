üéÅ Cat Packs

Packs are tiered reward bundles accessed via the /packs command. Each pack starts at one of eight tiers and has a **30% chance to upgrade** its rarity every step, meaning even a Wooden Pack can potentially become a Celestial Pack!

Pack Value Table

The Result Value is the theoretical average value of cats you can expect to receive after factoring in the 30% upgrade chance.

**Result Value Formula:** \text{Base Value} \times (1 - \text{Upgrade Chance}) + (\text{Upgrade Chance} \times \text{Result Value of the pack a rarity above})

The Reward Algorithm (Recap)

This is a summary of the steps taken to determine the final reward when you open a pack:

  1. A pack starts with its initial rarity tier.
  2. **Rarity Check:** There is a **70% chance** to keep the current rarity (proceed to step 3) or a **30% chance** to upgrade and repeat this step (unless it's Celestial).
  3. Once the final rarity is chosen, its **Base Value** is fetched.
  4. A random cat type is picked (1/22 chance each).
  5. The number of cats is calculated: Pack Base Value / Cat Type Value.
  6. The **whole number** of the result is guaranteed; the **fractional part** is the chance of receiving an extra cat.
  7. If the result is less than 1, the reward falls back to **1 Fine cat**.
  8. If the total reward is nothing, a special cutscene occurs for excitement!
Packs were introduced in **Season 3 Cattlepass**! They replaced the old "5 random cats" reward with a Stone Pack to provide more valuable and exciting rewards.
